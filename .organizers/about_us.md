**Welcome to Golang Warsaw!**

Our mission is to cultivate a vibrant community for Go developers in Warsaw. This is your meetup regardless whether you're a seasoned Go developer or just starting your journey. We host speakers of all experience levels, from lightning talks to in-depth presentations. Have something to share? Want to connect with fellow engineers? Join our community.

**What to expect:**

- **Talks for all levels:** Whether you're a seasoned Go developer or just starting your journey, you'll find valuable insights and discussions.
- **Your stage:** Have something to share? This is your platform! We welcome first-time speakers and are happy to support you in preparing your talk.
- **Networking:** Connect with fellow Go enthusiasts, share ideas, and build lasting relationships.

Join us to learn, share, and grow with the Warsaw Go community. Let us know if you'd like to speak or have any questions.
